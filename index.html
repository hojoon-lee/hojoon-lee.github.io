About Me

I am currently an assistant professor at Dept. of Computer Science and Engineering at Sungkyunkwan University since September, 2019. Before that, I spent one year as a postdoctoral researcher at CISPA under supervison of Prof. Michael Backes. I recevied my Ph.D from KAIST in 2018 and my advisor was Prof. Brent Byunghoon Kang. I received my B.S. from The University of Texas at Austin.

Research Interests

My main research interests lie in retrofitting security in computing systems against today’s advanced threats. My research interests include but not limited to:
  • Operating System Security
  • Trusted Execution Environments
  • Program Analysis
  • Software Fault Isolation
  • Secure Computation in Cloud
